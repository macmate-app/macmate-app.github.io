Icon Builder Plus

Introducing Icon Builder Plus, the ultimate macOS app for creating stunning icons! With this app, you don't need to have any design skills to create amazing icons that are sure to grab everyone's attention.

With its user-friendly interface, you can easily start from scratch or choose from a variety of open-source free icons such as Font Awesome, Material, Feather, Bootstrap, and Ionicons. You can customize each element to your liking with ease, including squares, circles, icons, and texts. You can change the size, color, position, and more!

The live preview feature lets you see the changes you make immediately, ensuring that you get exactly what you want. You can save your icons to your hard disk, load them later, and work on multiple icons at the same time in different windows/documents.

Icon Builder Plus also lets you export your icons to PNG and SVG files, making it easy to use them on any platform. You can toggle between dark and light themes, or set it to auto-switch based on your system theme. You can also toggle guides to help you align your elements perfectly.

Whether you need icons for your website, app, or any other project, Icon Builder Plus is the perfect tool to help you create stunning icons with ease. Try it out now and see for yourself how easy it is to create beautiful icons!
