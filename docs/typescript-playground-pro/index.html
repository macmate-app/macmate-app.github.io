TypeScript Playground Pro

Introducing TypeScript Playground Pro, the must-have macOS app for TypeScript developers and learners alike! With TypeScript Playground Pro, you can easily run TypeScript snippets, write and test new code, and quickly identify and fix errors and warnings.

Our app comes packed with powerful features that will make your coding experience a breeze. With linting support, errors and warnings are highlighted, making it easy to spot and fix issues. And with auto-fix, most errors and warnings are resolved automatically upon saving, saving you valuable time and effort.

But that's not all. TypeScript Playground Pro also includes auto-formatting powered by Prettier, ensuring that your code is always neat and organized. Whether you're just getting started with TypeScript or looking to try out new ideas, TypeScript Playground Pro is the perfect tool to help you learn, test, and iterate quickly.

And with realtime output displayed in the right panel, you can see the results of your code instantly, making it easier than ever to test and refine your TypeScript skills.

So why wait? Download TypeScript Playground Pro today and experience the power and convenience of the ultimate TypeScript development tool!
