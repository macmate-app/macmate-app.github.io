TypeScript Playground Pro

Introducing TypeScript Playground Pro - the ultimate app for TypeScript enthusiasts! Say goodbye to the hassle of setting up TypeScript environments and hello to hassle-free coding with our easy-to-use macOS app.

Our app allows you to run TypeScript snippets instantly without any installation or configuration required. With linting support, you'll never miss a warning or error. Plus, with our auto-fix feature, most errors will be resolved upon saving, saving you time and frustration.

Formatting your code has never been easier with our built-in Prettier integration. Just save your code and let our app take care of the rest. Our GUI is divided into two parts, with the left side featuring the powerful Monaco editor used in VS Code, and the right side displaying code output in real-time.

With our app, you're in control. Terminate the running of the code with a simple click of a button. So why wait? Download TypeScript Playground Pro today and experience the power of hassle-free TypeScript coding!
